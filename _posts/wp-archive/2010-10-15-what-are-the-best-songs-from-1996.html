--- layout: post title: What are the best songs from 1996? tags: - random status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_lock: '1287182206' _edit_last: '1' --- The eternal question.  I'm sure you've debated this question amongst your friends and family.  Right??? Best from the Billboard Top 100 of 1996, more specifically. Something to keep in mind:  My older sister bought a lot of CDs this year...

My list (in somewhat of a particular order):

More obscure artists / 1 hit wonders:
  • Donna Lewis - Always Forever
  • Jann Arden - Insensitive
  • Sophie B Hawkins - As I Lay Me Down
  • Merril Bainbridge - Mouth
  • Everything But the Girl - Missing
  • Dishwalla - Counting Blue Cars
  • Bodeans - Closer to Free
  • Ginuwine - Pony (mostly because it's so ridiculous)
More widely-known:
  • Natalie Merchant - Wonder
  • Mariah Carey ft. Boyz II Men - One Sweet Day
  • Mariah Carey - Fantasy
  • Hootie and the Blowfish - Only Wanna Be With You
  • Seal - Don't Cry
  • Gin Blossoms - Follow You Down
  • Oasis - Wonderwall
  • Blues Traveler - Hook
I'm considering doing a YouTube series of some of these songs next year, which just happens to be 15 years after these tunes were hits.  Maybe I could continue with that offset (upload covers of 1997 songs in 2012, etc.).  DISCLAIMER: Like most other projects I dream up, this one probably won't come to fruition.
